Most of the recent computational neuroscience work has been done in the Neurosim Lab at SUNY Downstate Medical Center.

From 2005-2010, work in computational neuroscience was performed within the Complex Systems Group at the University of Sydney; starting in September 2014, the Kerr Lab will resume residency there.

The computational methods developed by the Kerr Lab have found a corporate use via Cingulate Consulting, a "big data" analytics start-up.
